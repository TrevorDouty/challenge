Welcome to Bad Golfer! The site for bad golfers. You might be here because you are a good golfer and want to watch bad golfers or you are a bad golfer looking for tips. Either way we got you covered!

This is how you know you are a bad golfer!

Are you that guy that has to always buy a new club because this will finally be the club to fix your swing? Maybe you you spend more time going left and right down the course than forwards.. Or maybe you are just that guy that thinks you need to be playing classic rock as loud as you can while you play. This is the start to being a bad golfer.

  • You can't ever find your ball
  • You spend too much money on your clothes to look like you fit in
  • The grass flies farther than your ball
  • The safest place on the course is behind the flag
  • You somehow manage to hurt yourself golfing
